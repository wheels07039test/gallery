About These Drawings

I drew all of these between when I was in 6th grade and when I was a senior in High School. At the time that I drew these, I was not confident enough to be able to make my own designs for what I drew, so I always tried to replicate another image to the best of my ability to get used to drawing. The goal was to eventually be able to draw my own images that weren't just carbon copies of other images, but I never really got that far before I stopped drawing.
One thing that I would like to make clear, however, is that I do not trace art. All of these were created by me having the image up on my phone or computer and very carefully drawing an exact replica of it just by looking at it. That is all. Enjoy.
